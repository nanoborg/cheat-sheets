# Ratio

### Ratio Between Sets of Numbers

Find the __Highest__ Common Factor

    6 -> Factors 1,2,3,6
    10 -> Factors 1,2,5,10

    The Highest Common Factor is 2

    6 / 2 = 3
    10 / 2 = 5

    Ratio 3:5
